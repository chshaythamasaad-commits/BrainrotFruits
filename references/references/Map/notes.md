# BrainrotFruits Map Reference Notes

Use brainrotfruits_map_reference.png as the main visual/layout reference for the BrainrotFruits map.

Goal:
- colorful voxel island hub
- 6 player plots around the outside
- central safe zone
- shared central catapult/launch area
- reveal zone at the end of the launch lane
- shop, sell, upgrades, and index stands around the hub
- rarest fruit statue/showcase in the center
- paths, fences, trees, rocks, lamps, water, small cliffs, and island edges

Do not directly copy another game's exact map or assets.
Use the reference for layout, readability, color, and simulator-style structure only.

Important player-readability goals:
1. Player should instantly understand where their plot is.
2. Player should instantly understand where the shared launch area is.
3. Player should instantly understand where the reveal zone is.
4. Player should instantly understand where shop, sell, upgrades, and index are.

Visual style:
- blocky Roblox simulator island
- colorful and clean
- voxel/block-built
- polished but simple
- readable from a zoomed-out camera
- not flat or empty
- not cluttered with overlapping labels

Map structure:
- central plaza/safe zone
- 6 plots arranged around the outside
- each plot has fruit pads, spawn pad, fence, owner sign, and small decoration
- shared launch lane starts near the center and extends forward
- reveal zone sits at the end of the lane
- rarest fruit showcase statue sits in the hub
