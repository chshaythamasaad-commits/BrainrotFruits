# Golden Strawberita

Base character:
- Strawberita

Audience:
- PG / Roblox-safe / child-friendly

Variant type:
- Rare shiny variant

Visual direction:
- golden strawberry body
- shiny yellow/gold seeds
- leaf crown remains green but with gold trim
- add small golden sparkle particles
- keep cute chibi mascot shape
- no adult proportions
- no revealing outfit
- no suggestive pose

Gameplay direction:
- 2x or 3x income compared to base Strawberita
- easier to get than Galaxy or Diamond

Roblox implementation:
- part-based model
- use gold/yellow materials and sparkle effects
- readable from far away
- optimized for mobile
