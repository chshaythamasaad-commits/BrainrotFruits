# Coconutto Bonkini

## Style Direction

Use the voxel/chibi Roblox-friendly style from the reference image.

The model should look like a cute collectible fruit character:
- Large oversized fruit head/body
- Small chibi body
- Blocky voxel construction
- Simple square forms
- Bright colors
- Cute readable face
- Mobile-friendly geometry
- Easy to animate

Do not make him realistic or aggressive. He should be silly and lovable.

## Base Design

Coconutto Bonkini is a coconut caveman character.

Important visual features:
- Large round coconut-brown head/body
- Cream/white coconut top or inner coconut detail
- Simple crack lines on coconut shell
- Cute square face panel
- Big eyes
- Blush cheeks
- Small smile or silly grin
- Caveman tunic/skirt
- Brown/yellow primitive spotted outfit
- Small wooden club accessory or club motif
- Short blocky limbs
- Chunky simple feet/boots

The coconut shell shape should be the most readable part.

## Personality

Silly, strong, lovable, dumb-but-cute caveman energy.

He should feel like the funny early-game bruiser character.

## Roblox Implementation Notes

Start with simple placeholder geometry:
- Use blocky Parts/MeshParts
- Coconut head should be round-ish but voxel/blocky
- Use brown blocks for shell
- Use cream blocks for coconut interior/top
- Use darker thin blocks or decals for cracks
- Club can be a simple brown cylinder/block shape
- Outfit should be a simple jagged skirt/tunic shape

Keep the club small enough that it does not ruin the silhouette.

## Animation Notes

Idle animation ideas:
- Raises club happily
- Scratches head
- Small stomp
- Belly/body bounce
- Silly grin pose

Landing animation:
- Drops heavily
- Club bonks the ground
- Small dust puff or impact ring

## Variants

Base:
- Normal brown coconut shell
- Cream coconut top
- Caveman outfit

Golden:
- Coconut shell becomes golden brown/gold
- Club becomes shiny gold or polished wood
- Add coin sparkle particles

Rainbow:
- Coconut shell gets rainbow color cycling
- Outfit stays simple
- Add rainbow sparkle trail

Toxic:
- Coconut becomes rotten green/brown
- Cracks glow green/purple
- Add slime bubbles or toxic drips

Cosmic:
- Coconut shell becomes dark purple/blue
- Cracks become glowing star lines
- Add small star particles
