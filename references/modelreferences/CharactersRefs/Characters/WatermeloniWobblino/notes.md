# Watermeloni Wobblino

## Style Direction

Use the voxel/chibi Roblox-friendly style from the reference image.

The model should look like a cute collectible fruit character:
- Large oversized fruit body/head
- Small sturdy limbs
- Blocky voxel construction
- Simple square forms
- Bright saturated colors
- Cute readable face
- Mobile-friendly geometry
- Easy to animate

Do not make him too realistic or too muscular. Keep him round, cute, and chunky.

## Base Design

Watermeloni Wobblino is a watermelon sumo/tank character.

Important visual features:
- Large round green watermelon head/body
- Dark green stripe pattern
- Small green stem on top
- Cute square face panel
- Big eyes
- Blush cheeks
- Small happy mouth
- Red sumo belt/mawashi
- Very squat proportions
- Tiny sturdy legs
- Short chunky arms
- Small blocky feet

The round watermelon silhouette is the most important part.

## Personality

Heavy, goofy, tanky, cheerful.

He should feel slow but valuable and strong.

## Roblox Implementation Notes

Start with simple placeholder geometry:
- Use blocky Parts/MeshParts
- Body/head should be large and round-ish but voxel-like
- Use green blocks for base
- Use darker green blocks/stripes for watermelon pattern
- Sumo belt should be a simple red wrap around the body
- Keep limbs very short and chunky

Make sure the character does not become too large for plot placement.

## Animation Notes

Idle animation ideas:
- Belly bounce
- Small stomp
- Slow side-to-side wobble
- Flexes tiny arms
- Blinks happily

Landing animation:
- Heavy drop
- Small shockwave ring
- Bounces once like a watermelon ball

## Variants

Base:
- Normal green watermelon colors
- Red sumo belt

Golden:
- Watermelon becomes gold/green-gold
- Belt becomes red/gold
- Add coin sparkle particles

Rainbow:
- Watermelon stripes cycle rainbow colors
- Add rainbow ring effect

Toxic:
- Watermelon becomes toxic green/purple
- Stripes glow neon
- Add bubbling slime particles

Cosmic:
- Watermelon becomes dark galaxy green/blue/purple
- Stripes become starry glowing lines
- Add small star particles
