# Dragonfrutto Drippo

## Style Direction

Use the voxel/chibi Roblox-friendly style from the reference image.

The model should look like a cute collectible fruit character:
- Large oversized fruit head/body
- Small chibi body
- Blocky voxel construction
- Simple square forms
- Bright saturated colors
- Cute confident face
- Mobile-friendly geometry
- Easy to animate

Do not overcomplicate the outfit. Keep it stylish but readable.

## Base Design

Dragonfrutto Drippo is a cool dragon fruit influencer/drip character.

Important visual features:
- Large vivid pink dragon fruit head/body
- Black seed dots across the fruit surface
- Green leafy spike accents
- Cute square face panel
- Confident smile
- Black sunglasses or cool shades
- Simple stylish outfit
- Small chain or gold detail if easy
- Chunky shoes
- Short blocky arms and legs

The pink fruit body, green spikes, and sunglasses are the most important features.

## Personality

Flashy, confident, stylish, arrogant but funny.

He should feel like a high-rarity chase character.

## Roblox Implementation Notes

Start with simple placeholder geometry:
- Use blocky Parts/MeshParts
- Dragon fruit head/body should be pink and large
- Add black square seed dots
- Use green blocky spikes/leaves around head/body
- Sunglasses should be simple black blocks/decals
- Outfit should use clean dark/pink blocks
- Shoes should be chunky and stylish

Avoid adding too many spike pieces if performance becomes an issue.

## Animation Notes

Idle animation ideas:
- Adjusts sunglasses
- Tiny flex/pose
- Head nod
- Cool side lean
- Shoe tap

Landing animation:
- Lands in confident pose
- Adjusts shades
- Sparkle flash or camera-flash effect

## Variants

Base:
- Normal vivid pink dragon fruit colors
- Green spikes
- Black sunglasses

Golden:
- Pink body becomes gold/pink-gold
- Sunglasses stay black
- Add coin sparkle particles

Rainbow:
- Pink body cycles rainbow colors
- Spikes stay green or cycle slightly
- Add rainbow sparkle effect

Toxic:
- Pink becomes toxic purple/green
- Spikes glow neon green
- Add slime bubbles or toxic mist

Cosmic:
- Body becomes dark purple/blue galaxy palette
- Seeds become tiny stars
- Sunglasses can glow
- Add star particles and cosmic sparkle
