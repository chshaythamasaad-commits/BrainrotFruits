# Lemonaldo Sprintini

## Style Direction

Use the voxel/chibi Roblox-friendly style from the reference image.

The model should look like a cute collectible fruit character:
- Large oversized fruit head
- Small chibi body
- Blocky voxel construction
- Simple square forms
- Bright saturated colors
- Cute expressive face
- Mobile-friendly geometry
- Easy to animate

Do not make the sports outfit too detailed. Keep it clean and readable.

## Base Design

Lemonaldo Sprintini is a lemon athlete/sprinter character.

Important visual features:
- Large bright yellow lemon-shaped head
- Small green leaf/stem on top
- Lemon skin dots or small square bumps
- Cute determined face
- Big eyes
- Angled eyebrows
- Sporty headband
- Athletic shirt or tank top
- Shorts
- Striped socks
- Chunky sneaker-like shoes
- Short blocky arms and legs

The lemon shape and sporty runner outfit should be readable from far away.

## Personality

Fast, cocky, competitive, energetic.

He should feel like the speed-focused rare character.

## Roblox Implementation Notes

Start with simple placeholder geometry:
- Use blocky Parts/MeshParts
- Lemon head should be round/oval but voxel-like
- Headband can be a simple white/green band
- Outfit should use simple torso color blocks
- Shoes should be oversized blocky sneakers
- Keep legs slightly more dynamic than other characters if possible

Avoid tiny shoe details that are hard to see in-game.

## Animation Notes

Idle animation ideas:
- Jogging in place
- Stretching
- Fast foot taps
- Headband adjustment
- Competitive bounce

Landing animation:
- Lands in runner stance
- Does tiny sprint burst
- Leaves a short dust/speed trail

## Variants

Base:
- Normal bright yellow lemon colors
- White/green athletic outfit

Golden:
- Lemon body becomes golden yellow
- Shoes get gold accents
- Add coin sparkle particles

Rainbow:
- Lemon body or outfit cycles rainbow colors
- Add rainbow speed trail

Toxic:
- Lemon becomes neon green/yellow
- Add sour slime effects
- Small bubbling particles

Cosmic:
- Lemon becomes dark blue/purple galaxy palette
- Leaf/stem can glow
- Add star particles and cosmic speed trail
