# Strawberita

## Style Direction

Use the voxel/chibi Roblox-friendly style from the reference image.

The model should look like a cute collectible fruit character:
- Large oversized fruit head
- Small chibi body
- Blocky voxel construction
- Simple square forms
- Bright saturated colors
- Cute kawaii face
- Mobile-friendly geometry
- Easy to animate

Do not use smooth realistic proportions. Do not use overly detailed meshes.

## Base Design

Strawberita is the main strawberry mascot character.

Important visual features:
- Large strawberry-shaped red head
- Yellow square seed dots across the strawberry head
- Green leafy top/crown
- Small green stem
- Pink bow near the top/side of the head
- Square face panel
- Big cute eyes
- Blush cheeks
- Small happy mouth
- Small chibi body
- Red strawberry-themed dress/outfit
- White trim on skirt
- Pink/white striped socks
- Red shoes

## Personality

Cute, energetic, mascot-like, slightly idol/star energy.

She should feel like the first recognizable face of the game.

## Roblox Implementation Notes

Start with simple placeholder geometry:
- Use blocky Parts/MeshParts
- Keep the head much larger than the body
- Keep limbs short and simple
- Use decals or simple surfaces for face details
- Use small square/flat pieces for seeds
- Use simple leaf blocks for the top crown

The final model should be easy to replace later with a proper imported mesh.

## Animation Notes

Idle animation ideas:
- Small bounce
- Head tilt
- Tiny wave
- Bow/leaf wiggle
- Cute side-to-side sway

Landing animation:
- Pops out of fruit/crate
- Bounces once
- Waves or poses

## Variants

Base:
- Normal red strawberry colors

Golden:
- Red parts become gold/yellow
- Seeds become brighter white/gold
- Add light coin sparkle particles

Rainbow:
- Body cycles between rainbow colors
- Keep green leaves readable
- Add soft rainbow trail or sparkle

Toxic:
- Red becomes green/purple slime palette
- Seeds become neon green or purple
- Add small bubbling particles

Cosmic:
- Body becomes dark purple/blue galaxy palette
- Seeds become tiny star dots
- Add small star particles
