# Bananito Bandito

## Style Direction

Use the voxel/chibi Roblox-friendly style from the reference image.

The model should look like a cute collectible fruit character:
- Large oversized fruit head/body
- Small chibi body
- Blocky voxel construction
- Simple square forms
- Bright saturated colors
- Cute readable face
- Mobile-friendly geometry
- Easy to animate

Do not use realistic cowboy proportions. Keep it toy-like and simple.

## Base Design

Bananito Bandito is a banana cowboy/bandit character.

Important visual features:
- Large banana-yellow head/body
- Banana peel shape or peel crown
- Cute square face panel
- Big friendly eyes
- Small mischievous smile
- Brown cowboy hat
- Red or brown bandana
- Simple belt with buckle
- Brown boots
- Small western/bandit details
- Short blocky arms and legs

Keep the cowboy details simple. The banana silhouette should still be the main thing.

## Personality

Sneaky, playful, dramatic, little outlaw energy.

He should feel funny and mischievous, not scary.

## Roblox Implementation Notes

Start with simple placeholder geometry:
- Use blocky Parts/MeshParts
- Head/body should be banana-shaped or tall rounded blocky form
- Hat can be made from simple brown blocks
- Bandana can be a red block/flat piece around the neck
- Belt and buckle should be simple cuboids
- Boots should be chunky brown blocks

Avoid tiny accessories that are hard to see from far away.

## Animation Notes

Idle animation ideas:
- Tips hat
- Looks left and right suspiciously
- Tiny side-step dance
- Hand on belt pose
- Finger-gun pose if possible

Landing animation:
- Rolls or stumbles out
- Hat bounces slightly
- Strikes a bandit pose

## Variants

Base:
- Normal yellow banana colors
- Brown cowboy accessories

Golden:
- Banana body becomes shiny gold
- Belt buckle becomes brighter
- Add coin sparkle particles

Rainbow:
- Banana body gets rainbow gradient/color cycling
- Keep cowboy hat brown for readability
- Add rainbow sparkle trail

Toxic:
- Banana becomes green/yellow rotten palette
- Add purple/green slime details
- Add small bubbling particles

Cosmic:
- Banana becomes dark blue/purple cosmic palette
- Hat can become black/dark purple
- Add small star particles
